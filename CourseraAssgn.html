Get to know Interesting facts about Planets!!!

MARS

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, being only larger than Mercury. In English, Mars carries the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred to as the "Red Planet"

SATURN

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System. One cannot stand on Saturn because it is made mostly of gases. It has a lot of helium. This is the same kind of gas that you put in balloons.

PLUTO

Pluto will always be the ninth planet to us! Smaller than Earth's moon, Pluto was a planet up until 2006 and has five of its own moons! The planet Pluto is named after the Roman god of the underworld. It was the first and the largest Kuiper belt object to be discovered. Pluto was discovered by Clyde Tombaugh in 1930.
